Can Sean Draw it

Thank you for visiting my site! I love art, but struggle with coming up with things to draw. This site is a submission based site where you can submit an idea for me to draw to help me practice my art skills.

So the layout of this website is very simple, at the end of the rules will be a link to the next page. On this page you will fill out a form with your name, email, and a description of what I should draw. When I am done drawing your picture, I will send you an email telling you that it is posted. Posted images can be found on the gallery page (link can be found on the Form page).

There are only two rules of this site. The first one is please keep your submissions PG-13. Anyone has access to this site and I would feel terrible if they stumbled upon something inappropriate. The second rule is to please only submit one idea at a time (i.e. wait for your first drawing to be posted before you submit a second). Enjoy
